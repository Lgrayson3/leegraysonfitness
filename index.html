About

Movement specialist serving Southeast Michigan

I've spent twelve years helping people move better. My clients have gone from limited mobility to running marathons, from chronic pain to pain-free living, from being afraid to move to trusting their bodies again.

I specialize in functional mobility and corrective exercise. That means I look at how your body actually moves, identify the patterns causing your pain or limitation, and build a program that fixes the root cause instead of chasing the symptom.

I work with aging adults, post-rehab patients, desk workers, beginners, and everyone in between. I train you in your home with a program built specifically for your body. No gym membership or special equipment required.

Services

How I work with clients

Every program starts with understanding how your body moves. From there, we build.

Movement Assessment

A comprehensive evaluation of how you move, where you're restricted, and what's driving your pain or limitation. This is the roadmap for everything that follows.

Corrective Exercise

Targeted work to address dysfunctional movement patterns, muscle imbalances, and compensation. We find what's not working and systematically rebuild it.

Strength and Conditioning

Progressive training built on a foundation of movement quality. You get stronger because you move well first, and every exercise is earned through proper mechanics.
