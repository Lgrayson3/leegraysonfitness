The 5 Most Common Issues I Treat

Chronic pain and movement dysfunction in adults usually traces back to one of these.

Most of my clients in Ann Arbor come to me with at least one. Many have been managing it for years without the right intervention.

Knee Pain
Osteoarthritis, ACL recovery, valgus collapse, patellar tracking
Back Pain
Chronic low back pain, disc degeneration, nerve involvement
Shoulder Injury
Instability, impingement, rotator cuff compensation
Ankle Instability
Chronic sprains, Achilles tendinopathy, dorsiflexion loss
Hip Impingement
FAI, labral wear, flexor restriction, guarding patterns

Left alone, these patterns compound. Compensations travel up and down the chain. The earlier you address them, the simpler the fix.

Corrective Exercise Services

I pick up exactly where physical therapy leaves off.

Physical therapy resolves the acute issue. I rebuild functional strength, joint stability, and movement confidence on top of that foundation. Every session is designed around your body and your assessment findings.

Load Management

Movement Quality Assessment

Functional screening to identify compensations, asymmetries, and dysfunction. I correct the underlying pattern, not the surface-level symptom.

Range of Motion Restoration

About Your Trainer

Lee Grayson

I’ve been training people for over 12 years, from big box gym floors to private studios to the setup I run now: one-on-one corrective exercise, delivered to your home across Ann Arbor and Southeast Michigan.
